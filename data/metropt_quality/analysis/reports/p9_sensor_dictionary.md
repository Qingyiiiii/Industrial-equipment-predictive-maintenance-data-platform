# P9 Sensor Dictionary

## Scope

- Dataset: `MetroPT-3 Dataset`.
- Local source checked by worker: `<WORKER_PROJECT_ROOT>/datas/MetroPT3_AirCompressor.csv`.
- Sensor count: `7` analog sensors and `8` digital sensors.
- `DV_eletric` is the original CSV spelling. The project standard field is `dv_electric`.

The data description reports `15,169,480 data points`; the current project ODS baseline is `1,516,948` timestamp rows. These are different counting concepts and must not be mixed in model reports.

## Sensor Fields

| # | Type | Raw field | Standard field | Unit/value | Physical meaning | Modeling note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | analog | `TP2` | `tp2` | bar | Pressure measured on the compressor. | Compressor-side pressure; useful for pressure generation and leak-behavior analysis. |
| 2 | analog | `TP3` | `tp3` | bar | Pressure generated at the pneumatic panel. | Should stay close to reservoir pressure under stable operation. |
| 3 | analog | `H1` | `h1` | bar | Pressure generated by the pressure drop during cyclonic separator discharge. | Useful for discharge behavior and pressure-drop anomaly analysis. |
| 4 | analog | `DV_pressure` | `dv_pressure` | bar | Pressure drop when air-dryer towers discharge. | Pairs with valve/electric signals to explain dryer discharge and loaded states. |
| 5 | analog | `Reservoirs` | `reservoirs` | bar | Downstream pressure of the reservoirs. | Supports pressure-balance features such as tp3 - reservoirs. |
| 6 | analog | `Motor_current` | `motor_current` | A | Current of one phase of the three-phase motor. | Used to derive operating_state and explain load/offload cycles. |
| 7 | analog | `Oil_temperature` | `oil_temperature` | C | Oil temperature on the compressor. | Thermal stress indicator; historically shows a large failure-window mean shift. |
| 8 | digital | `COMP` | `comp` | 0/1 | Electrical signal of the air-intake valve on the compressor. | Useful with MPG and motor_current for state-machine behavior. |
| 9 | digital | `DV_eletric` | `dv_electric` | 0/1 | Electrical signal controlling the compressor outlet valve. | Original CSV spelling is DV_eletric; the project standardizes it as dv_electric. |
| 10 | digital | `Towers` | `towers` | 0/1 | Signal defining which drying tower is operating and which is draining humidity. | Supports dryer-cycle and tower-switching analysis. |
| 11 | digital | `MPG` | `mpg` | 0/1 | Signal that starts the compressor under load by activating the intake valve. | Often shares behavior with COMP and indicates pressure-recovery demand. |
| 12 | digital | `LPS` | `lps` | 0/1 | Low-pressure switch signal. | Rare but operationally important low-pressure event indicator. |
| 13 | digital | `Pressure_switch` | `pressure_switch` | 0/1 | Electrical signal detecting discharge in the air-drying towers. | Pairs with DV pressure and towers for dryer-discharge diagnostics. |

| 15 | digital | `Caudal_impulses` | `caudal_impulses` | 0/1 | Pulse output generated by the amount of air flowing from APU to reservoirs. | Useful for flow activity and state-transition features. |

## Modeling Boundary

- Analog sensors can be used for rolling statistics, slopes, pressure differences, and correlation analysis.
- Digital sensors should be treated as binary state/control signals, with activation counts, toggles, and duration features.
- `operating_state` is derived from `motor_current`: `loaded` when `motor_current >= 7.0`, `unloaded` when `1.0 <= motor_current < 7.0`, and `stopped` otherwise.
